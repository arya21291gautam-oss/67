Who We Are

A Family Built on Care

A&R Cares was founded by two registered nurses who saw, firsthand, the difference that truly personalised care can make in someone’s life. After years working in hospitals and healthcare settings across Australia, we decided to bring that clinical knowledge — and our personal values — into the community.

As a Nepali-owned, family-run business, we understand what it means to care for people you love. We bring that same warmth and dedication to every participant we support. For us, this isn’t just a job — it’s a calling.

We speak English, Nepali, and Hindi, which means we can communicate meaningfully with participants and families from diverse backgrounds. We understand cultural values around care, family, and respect — and we honour them in everything we do.
